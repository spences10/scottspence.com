Macros

What are macros?

Macros make up the calorie content of food:

- 1 gram of fat = 9 calories
- 1 gram of carbs (carbohydrates) = 4 calories
- 1 gram of protein = 4 calories

Don't treat any of them as bad, there's no 'bad' macros
